Strawberry Shortcake

Ingredients

  • 1 quart fresh strawberries
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 package prepared sponge cake
  • 1 can whipped cream

Directions

  1. Place strawberries in a container with a lid; add sugar and stir to coat. Place lid on the container and refrigerate until sugar has dissolved, at least 15 minutes.
  2. Place a 1 dessert cup in each serving bowl and smother with strawberries. Top each with whipped cream.
  3. To have warm place in oven for 5 minutes at 375oF.
