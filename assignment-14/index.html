The Photographer



Morgan is a north idaho native who loves the outdoors and photography. Morgan started taking photos in highschool for fun and for the yearbook committee, she later expanded her art to other areas outside of school and focused on the overall outcome of her photos. Now her camera is by her side on every adventure. Her favorite photoshoot so far has been at Glacier National Park. Please enjoy her work as you browse through her world of photography.
